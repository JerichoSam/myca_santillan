Overview

Introduction

This project analyzes a retail sales dataset using Microsoft Excel. The dataset was provided by Great Learning as part of their Excel for Beginners certification program.

The course covers data cleaning, text manipulation, formulas, VLOOKUP/XLOOKUP, pivot tables, pivot charts, and dashboard visualization. The goal is to transform raw retail data into meaningful insights about sales trends, category performance, regional results, and discount impact.

Certified in Excel for Beginners from Great Learning upon completing this project.

Problem Statement

The Ask

The retail chain faces challenges in managing inventory, promotions, and regional performance. Management needs answers to:

  • Monthly and seasonal sales trends — when do peaks and dips occur?
  • Top-performing and underperforming product categories
  • Sales performance compared across different regions
  • The impact of discount strategies on total and net revenue

The objective is to surface insights that guide inventory planning, marketing strategies, pricing decisions, and regional resource allocation.

Methodology

Process

The dataset was cleaned, prepared, analyzed, and visualized entirely in Microsoft Excel across 7 steps:

1

Data Cleaning

Removed duplicates, handled missing values in Product / Category / Region, standardized text values, verified numeric fields, and ensured consistent OrderDate formatting.

  • Removed duplicate records
  • Handled missing values in Product, Category, Region columns
  • Standardized text values and verified numeric fields
  • Ensured OrderDate values were properly formatted
Data Cleaning — Raw Dataset View
2

Data Preprocessing

Created calculated columns to support time-series and revenue analysis:

  • TotalSales = Quantity × UnitPrice
  • NetSales = TotalSales − Discount
  • Month-Year column extracted from OrderDate
  • Quarter-Year column extracted from OrderDate

Interactive Dashboard

Insights

Recommendations

📈

Sales Trends

Investigate the mid-year dip in July 2024. Introduce seasonal promotions during low months to stabilize revenue throughout the year.

🗺️

Regional Performance

Analyze East region's 2024 decline. Replicate successful South region strategies in weaker markets to close the performance gap.

📦

Category Strategy

Boost inventory and promotions for Home Appliances. Review pricing and marketing for low-performing Groceries to improve contribution.

🎯

Overall Focus

Target weak months, regions, and categories with data-backed campaigns. Expand what's working in high-performing segments.
